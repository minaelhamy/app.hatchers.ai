# Hatchers Founder System Guide

## Purpose
Hatchers OS is the founder-facing operating system. Founders should experience one product, even though several specialist tools power different workflows behind the scenes.

## System Roles

### Hatchers OS
- Primary founder workspace at `app.hatchers.ai`
- Handles founder login, desktop navigation, onboarding, assistant context, company profile, task flow, and cross-tool launches
- Acts as the subscription authority for founders
- Launches all specialist tools with signed founder sessions

### Atlas
- AI and strategy workspace
- Stores and uses company intelligence
- Helps with brand positioning, campaigns, content generation, creative direction, and founder support
- Surfaces:
  - Company Intelligence
  - Brand Studio
  - Campaign Studio
  - Atlas Agents

### LMS
- Learning and execution support workspace
- Used for learning plans, milestones, mentor support, and execution rhythm

### Bazaar
- Product-commerce engine
- Used for product stores, orders, store content, categories, product pages, and product-selling workflows

### Servio
- Service-commerce engine
- Used for services, bookings, working hours, staff, and service-selling workflows

## Founder Experience Rules
- Founders log in once through Hatchers OS
- Founders should automatically access Atlas, LMS, Bazaar, and Servio without separate manual login
- Founders should not be asked to buy separate plans inside tools
- Founders should use OS icons as shortcuts to tool workspaces
- Tool-specific sidebars and duplicate navigation should be hidden when they create confusion inside OS

## Company Intelligence
- Company Intelligence is the shared business brain for the ecosystem
- It should contain:
  - company summary
  - offer
  - ICP / target audience
  - differentiators
  - brand voice
  - content goals
  - growth blockers
  - strategic guidance
- Atlas uses this intelligence to generate better content and strategy
- Other tools should rely on the same intelligence wherever founder context is needed

## Founder Support Expectations For Atlas
Atlas should be able to help founders with:
- where to click and what to do next in Hatchers OS
- how LMS learning and execution flow works
- how to use Bazaar for product selling
- how to use Servio for service selling
- how Company Intelligence affects campaigns, content, and websites
- what to prioritize to improve growth, conversions, and revenue

## Desktop Navigation Principles
- The OS desktop should emphasize clear founder actions rather than internal tool names wherever possible
- Tool names can still exist as direct engine apps:
  - Atlas
  - LMS
  - Bazaar
  - Servio
- Atlas-specific founder shortcuts should include:
  - Company Intelligence
  - Brand Studio
  - Campaign Studio
  - Atlas Agents

## Commerce Positioning
- Bazaar and Servio are both available to founders
- Founders can sell products, services, or both
- Access should not be restricted only because the founder started with one business model
